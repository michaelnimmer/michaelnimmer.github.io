About Me

Hello! I'm Michael. This is my professional website where I want to share a bit of my background, skills, and interests.

I'm an Actuarial Analyst currently advancing through the Society of Actuaries pathway. My day-to-day work involves financial analysis and risk assessment for clients.

Parallel to my actuarial career, I am planning to complete Master's degree in Data Science over the next few years. This will allow me to combine my analytical training with robust computational skills.

Focus & Vision

My core strengths lie in analytical problem-solving and understanding of complex systems. I excel at identifying underlying patterns and formulating effective, data-informed solutions.

In the long term, I hope to use my skills to contribute meaningfully to the improvement of systems and institutions... a very ambitious goal, I know! One area that seems ripe for innovation is the healthcare sector and I want to be on the cutting edge of such innovation.

My main hobbies outside of work include music (I am a former piano/guitar teacher), lifting weights, and reading widely on topics such as history and philosophy.
